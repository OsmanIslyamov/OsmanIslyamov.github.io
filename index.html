About myself

I am a software engineer specialized in Quality Assurance, with extensive experience in manual and automation testing across web, desktop, and mobile applications. I build and run test strategies across the full software development lifecycle for complex, high-traffic, and dynamic products.

For the past two years I have been a key QA contributor on a vehicle-focused mobile application, testing on both Android and iOS. I lead end-to-end manual testing, automation execution, test case design, and regression strategy, and I work closely with Front-End and Back-End teams to improve product quality and user experience.

I work in a microservices environment with a Flutter frontend and an Apollo GraphQL backend (router and subgraphs). My day-to-day tools include Swagger for API validation, Apollo GraphQL for queries and schema checks, Figma for design validation, GitLab for CI/CD, and modern AI-assisted development tools.

Throughout my career I have contributed to the design, testing, and delivery of more than seven products in international teams, combining technical QA expertise with project coordination and stakeholder communication.
